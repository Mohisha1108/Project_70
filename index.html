What's New

  • School Canteen

    PIS has two cafeterias: one for the Lower School students and one for the Middle and Upper School students.Students who are enrolled in the cafeteria program has received a healthy and satisfying meal every day. Our school maids prepare meals that are nutritionally balanced and pleasing to the children. They also integrate organic food when possible. Additionally, they continue to look for ways to ensure that our cafeteria is eco-friendly by reducing the use of plastic and other disposable items and provide a comprehensive and easy-to-use recycling system.Our Cafeteria gives us freshly made food and favors vegetables and seasonal fruits as well as organic products.

  • Online Examination system

    Online Exams Walk Through for the Podar International School Students. 1) The exam is to be attempted online 2) Combination of MCQ(Objective type) and Essay Questions 3) Students have the option of writing the essay questions in the notebook and scan/picture of the page and upload online. 4) Works best with Laptop/Desktop
